PhD Student in Neuro Imagery

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Applied Mathematics and Neuroimaging at the Institute of Neuroscience of La Timone, located in Marseille, France. Under the supervision of Guillaume Auzias and Julien Lefevre, I am a member of the MecA team.

My current research focuses on the morphometric analysis of the human cortex, in particular the development of algorithmic tools for the quantification of sulcal depth. Sulcal depth serves as a critical morphometric feature for cortical characterisation, enabling both clinical and methodological investigations.

Through this platform, I aim to share various resources related to image processing, mesh processing, applied mathematics and neuroimaging to promote knowledge exchange.
